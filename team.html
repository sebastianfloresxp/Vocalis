Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Jingying Xu

Jingying Xu is a globally minded youth leader, polyglot, and systems strategist committed to building the future infrastructure of equity and innovation for students worldwide.

Raised across five countries and fluent in four languages, Jingying brings cross-cultural fluency and institutional insight to her work. She currently serves as the Student Representative to the Ithaca Board of Education, advocating for approximately 1,400 students in district policy, and as the two-term President of FIRST Robotics Team 639, where she leads strategic, fundraising, and engineering operations.

Jingying has also conducted artificial intelligence research at Cornell University. Her honors include recognition from the Princeton Prize in Race Relations, the John Locke Essay Competition, and the Scholastic Writing Awards.

Website Design & Technical Systems

Sebastian Flores

Sebastian Flores is a student technologist, developer, and interdisciplinary problem-solver exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, computer science, and complex systems.

His work spans independent software development, data visualization, and large-scale network analysis. He has built technical projects examining global systems and interconnected datasets, including a network visualization mapping tens of thousands of firms and hundreds of thousands of relationships.

Sebastian has also conducted research at Cornell University and speaks four languages, bringing a multidisciplinary and global perspective to his work. He is particularly interested in using computation to transform complex data into intuitive and accessible digital experiences.
